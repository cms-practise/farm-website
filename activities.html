Our petting zoo

In our petting zoo, children can have fun feeding and petting farm animals and learning about different species. Our animals include:

  • Baby lambs
  • Baby goats
  • Pigs and piglets

Play Park

You can let your little ones run free in our exciting play park, a safe and welcoming space for children. Our play park has a variety of fun activities, including:

  • Slides
  • Swing sets
  • Climbing frames

Farm walks

Take a leisurely stroll through our farm and learn about sustainable farming practices while getting a glimpse into rural life. Our farm offers:

  • An apple orchard
  • Wildlife and diverse plant species
  • Fresh air and beautiful scenery
